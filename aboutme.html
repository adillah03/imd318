Favorite Food

My favorite food is tauhu bergedil. I love it because of the combination of two favored elements of Malay cuisine: tauhu (tofu) and bergedil (potato patties). I fondly remember making this dish with tofu stuffed with mashed potatoes, spices, and sometimes minced meat or vegetables. The stuffed tofu is then deep-fried until crispy on the outside. It's a delicious and flavorful snack or side dish many in Malaysia enjoy, making it even more special to me."
